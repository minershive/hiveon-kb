- Antminer T17 **

- Antminer T15 **

- Antminer Z9/Z9-Mini
- Antminer X3
- Antminer E3

- Innosilicon T3H+/T3+/T2Th+/T2Thf+/T2Thl+/T2Th/T2Tz-30T/T2Thm/T2Thf/T2T+(32T)/T2Ts-26T/T2Ti-25T/T2T-24T
